Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BID4PENNY

BID4PENNY auctions work on a penny auction model where each bid costs a small amount (typically $0.50-$1.00). Each bid increases the auction price by a penny and extends the auction timer. The last person to bid when the timer reaches zero wins the item at the final auction price, which is typically much lower than retail value.

B4P is the native utility token of the BID4PENNY platform. It can be used to purchase bid credits at a discount, earn rewards for platform participation, stake for passive income, and vote on platform governance decisions. The token is built on the Ethereum blockchain as an ERC-20 token.

To get started, create an account on BID4PENNY, connect your crypto wallet, and purchase bid credits using B4P tokens, PayPal, or credit card. Once you have credits, you can browse active auctions and start bidding. We recommend starting with smaller auctions to understand the platform mechanics before participating in high-value auctions.

BID4PENNY differs from traditional auctions in several ways: each bid costs a small fee, bids increase the price by only a penny, the auction timer extends with each bid, and all transactions are recorded on the blockchain for transparency. Additionally, participants can earn crypto rewards even if they don't win the auction.

If you win an auction, you'll pay the final auction price (which is typically much lower than retail value) and receive the item shipped to your registered address. You'll also receive B4P token rewards for winning. All shipping costs are included in the auction, and most items ship within 5-7 business days.
