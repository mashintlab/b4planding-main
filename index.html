Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BID4PENNY

Users buy BIDs using crypto or fiat, then join live auctions. Each bid adds €0.01 to the price and resets a 15-second timer. Last bidder when timer hits zero wins the item.

B4P is the native token used to buy BIDs, stake for rewards, vote on platform governance, access perks, and redeem auction wins.

Create an account, connect your Web3 wallet (or use traditional login), deposit crypto or fiat, purchase BIDs, and start bidding.

It’s gamified, blockchain-powered, transparent, and offers token rewards. It ensures fairness through smart contracts and real-time leaderboards.

You can either get the item delivered (in the EU) or redeem it for BIDs or B4P tokens.

You get 80% of your used BIDs back, plus loyalty points and bonus BIDs for future participation—making every bid valuable.
